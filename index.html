Important Legal Notes

This calculator provides an estimate based on UAE Federal Decree-Law No. 33 of 2021. Actual gratuity may vary based on:

  • Unpaid Leave: Days without pay are excluded from service duration
  • Deductions: Employers may deduct loans, damages, or legally owed amounts
  • Gross Misconduct: Dismissal under Article 120 may forfeit gratuity entitlement
  • Contract Legacy: Older contracts may fall under pre-2022 provisions

This tool provides informational estimates only and does not constitute legal advice. For official rulings, consult MoHRE or a qualified legal professional.
